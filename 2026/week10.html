Goodyear Gila Monsters vs This is Mizerable

Result: Goodyear survives another heart-attack matchup, while This Is Mizerable fumbles the win streak.
Goodyear Gila Monsters 9 – 7 This is Mizerable

Goodyear's blood pressure continues rising as they squeek by with another very close win for the second week in a row by defeating This is Mizerable 9-7. Goodyear had a commanding lead early on in the week but This is Mizerable, despirately trying to keep their longest win streak in franchise history alive, clawed back and brought it within 0.5 points on Sunday. But thanks to what I believe to be a tough Saturday night, This is Mizerable forgot to set his lineup and it cost him a loss. Rookie move kid. We know you had some memes ready to go too if you won, too bad we gotta wait until next year.

Even though Goodyear's HR numbers aren't what we are used to, that didn't stop them from having a very solid hitting week with 97 hits, 52 runs, 35 walks, 11 stolen bases, a .289 average, and 152 total bases. Mizerable gave Goodyear a couple more grey hairs by taking advantage on the pitching side, posting a much better 2.78 ERA and 1.291 WHIP compared to Goodyear’s 4.55 ERA and 1.430 WHIP. He also grabbed quality starts and batting average against, holding opponents to a .233 BAA while Goodyear sat at .269. The problem was Goodyear still punched back with 61 strikeouts, 4 wins, and 7 net saves, which helped shut the door.

League takeaway: Goodyear is finding ways to win even when the matchup gets ugly, which is usually what good teams do. This Is Mizerable may have blown it, but the rest of the league should probably stop treating them like a free win.
