BTA Boyz vs John's Super Team

John’s Super Team didn’t just beat BTA Boyz — he turned their playoff hopes into a dream
John's Super Team 12 – 5.5 BTA Boyz

We know for a fact John's Super Team was looking for that legendary 16-0 sweep, but I'm sure he'll settle with a 11-5 win. But dude, things are getting a extremely sketchy for The BTA Boyz. Rumor has it they'll never win a game ever again. Espeically with that atrocious offense. John dominated on the offensive side, winning runs 51–40, HRs 21–11, RBIs 51–37, BBs 30–25, SBN 7–3, and TB 160–113. Jeepers my man, thats a real tough look.

BTA did have one thing going for them: the batting average was sick sitting at .282, compared to John’s .254. Cute little category win for The Kid. Unfortunately, batting average does not matter much when the other team is busy hitting 10 more homers and stacking almost 50 more total bases.

Pitching was closer, but John still found ways to pull away. Both teams finished with a 3.78 ERA, but John had the cleaner WHIP at 1.160, more wins, fewer losses, and more net saves/holds. BTA had more innings and more strikeouts, but also ate 7 losses. Guy might need to look into trading some pitching for hitting. Waiver wire might save the season. But what the fuck do i know? I'm sitting one seed ahead of The BTA Boyz.

League takeaway: John’s Super Team looks like a real contender at 5–2–1, while BTA Boyz falls deeper into the danger zone at 1–5–2. At this point, BTA is not chasing the playoffs — they are chasing respect.

Me So Heorny vs Dixon Cider

Result: Are the 2023 champs back?! Dixon Cider is looking mortal
Me So Heorny 9.5 – 6.5 Dixon Cider

Me So Heorny coming up with the absolute big win against our 2025 defending champ, Dixon Cider! This was a legit statement win for Billy and his sidekick Danny. Dixon Cider came in OOZING with defending champ aura, but Heorny came up with a piching week that makes a matchup feel unwinnable. These are the kinda pitching numbers champions put up baby! A 2.75 ERA, 0.979 WHIP, 8 QS, and .197 BAA is filthy. Literally nothing anyone can do. I'm pretty sure these were some of my exact pitching numbers over at St. Laurence actually, which is wild. But Dixon threw more innings with 86 IP, but unfortunately a lot of those innings just lead to getting cooked.

Dixon Cider's offense actually did its part. They won runs 32–29, RBIs 46–34, BBs 31–29, SBN 2 to -3, and TB 119–114, so this was not some full-team collapse. The bats kept them alive. The pitching staff just wasn't it this week. Which ya know what they say.... That's showbizz baby!!!!

Heorny’s offense was not explosive, but it was efficient enough, and the pitching carried the rest. Even with Dixon winning strikeouts 76–73 and pushing more volume, the ratios were a disaster: 5.13 ERA, 1.465 WHIP, and .274 BAA.

League takeaway: Me So Heorny proves to be like a real title threat, while Dixon Cider just proved that even defending champs can get dragged when their pitching staff folds. Dixon Cider needs that offense back into shape if they're looking to repeat
